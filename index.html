ITCHY SWEATER
An easy to use platform that allows you to purchase or swap giftcards for `Itchy Tokens`, so you never need to fake smile again or hurt family members feelings.
Tell Me More

Services

Creating the perfect gift for everyone, backed by blockchain technology!

E-Commerce

We believe the giftcard market is antiquated and not built to serve the mordern consumer, we are creating blockchain driven gift giving ecommerce platform where you can swap giftcards or purcase itchy tokens that are pegged to the UDST(Tether) stable coin to be used at our partnering retailers.

Cross-platform Technology

Built for mobile(Android/IOS) and via chromium browser extensions(Chrome, Edge, Brave, etc..) on the go when you need it via our app on demand.

Security

Via the cosmos hub and the solana payment ecosystem, all transactions will be managed and accounted for via the Itchy contract.

2022-2023 Road Map

Please check our our road map and stay up to date with our accomplishments.

  • March 2022

    Itchy Sweater Lives

    During this phase we have worked to create our Litepaper, build out our vision and have crafted our Marketing/Customer Hub.

  • July 2022

    Swap Beta Launch

    During this phase we will complete a beta for our Itchy Sweater swap, in which you will be able to swap UST stable coins for our Itchy Token. The first 10,000 wallets to do so will be considered WL for use of our alpha launch.

  • August 2022

    Itchy Token Alpha Airdrop

    Once the first 10,000 wallets have stake and locked 1,000 UST for a term no shorter than 1 year. Itchy Tokens which are pegged to 1USDT will begin the aidropping process to Users. These users will be the first users to test usage with out customer base. We are currently working to acquire partnerships with large consumer and retail business.
